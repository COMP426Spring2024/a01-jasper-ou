Biography

LeBron James, often considered one of the greatest basketball players in NBA history, has won multiple NBA championships and MVP awards. Most notably, Lebron is known for being the all time point leader, being the apart of the only team to win a championship after being down 3-1 in the NBA finals, and being the only player to have more than 30,000 points, 10,000 assists, and 10,000 rebounds.
