Biography

Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, often considered one of the greatest basketball players in NBA history, has won multiple NBA championships and MVP awards. His notable accomplishments include: being the all-time point leader for decades, winning 6 championships, having the most all-star appearances, and for having the most lethal NBA move in history.
